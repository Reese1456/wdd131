Buildings

Buildings are the foundation of your war economy. Workers construct them during the Strategic Phase. All new buildings are inactive the round they’re built — they become active at the start of your next turn. Every building must be placed within 6" of an existing friendly building.

Build Order Guide

A typical opening build order to get your economy and production running:

1
Generator 2 Ore — Start generating Energy immediately
2
Mine 2 Ore — Assign a worker for +2 Ore/round
3
Light Vehicle Factory 3 Ore, 1 Energy — Begin producing units
4
Turret 2 Ore, 1 Energy — Protect your workers & buildings
5
Research Center 2 Ore, 1 Energy — Unlock upgrades & Heavy units
6
Heavy Arms Factory 4 Ore, 2 Energy — Field the Vanguard Tank
