Special Rules Reference

Armored

Attackers suffer −1 to hit rolls against this unit. A normal 4+ becomes a 5+.

Quick Attack

After this unit attacks, it may use any remaining movement it did not use before attacking.

Ignores Armored

This unit’s attacks completely bypass the Armored special rule. Roll normally.
